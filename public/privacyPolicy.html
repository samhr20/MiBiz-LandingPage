1. Introduction #

Welcome to Uplinorr Plus, a business management application designed to help distributors and entrepreneurs manage leads, inventory, customers, payments, subscriptions, and business analytics. This Privacy Policy explains how Innozaic Services Pvt. Ltd. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Uplinorr Plus, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect #

We may collect and store the following categories of information:

A. Personal Information

  • Name, email address, phone number, profile photo (optional), and user/distributor ID.
  • Authentication credentials managed securely via Supabase authentication services.

B. Business Information

  • Prospect and customer details, lead notes.
  • Product, inventory, order, and payment records.
  • Subscription plan and billing details (transaction references, statuses).

C. Device & Usage Information

  • Device model, OS version, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • IP address, network status, log data, and crash diagnostics to improve the app.

3. How We Use Your Information #

We use the information we collect for purposes including, but not limited to:

  • Creating and managing your account and authentication.
  • Syncing and securely backing up your business data using Supabase.
  • Managing leads, inventory, orders, and payments.
  • Processing subscription billing and verifying payment status.
  • Sending account-related notifications and important updates.
  • Analyzing aggregated usage for product improvements and support.

4. Data Storage & Security #

We store your data using Supabase — a secure cloud backend. All data transmissions use HTTPS/TLS encryption. We implement access controls, authentication, and best-practice security measures to protect your information.

While we use reasonable security measures, no system is completely immune to risk. We regularly review our security practices.

5. Sharing of Information #

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data in limited circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers to deliver the service (e.g., payment processors such as Razorpay).
  • When legally required (subpoena, court order) or to prevent fraud and abuse.
  • To enforce our terms, protect our rights, users, or others.

All third parties are required to protect your data and may only process it under contractual obligations.

6. Payment Information #

Subscription and payment processing is handled by trusted gateways such as Razorpay. We do not store full card numbers or sensitive payment data on our servers. We retain payment transaction references and status details for billing records and customer support.

7. Your Rights #

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal or contractual retention obligations).
  • Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data where applicable.
  • Withdraw Consent: If processing is based on consent, you may withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@innozaic.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Data Retention #

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active, necessary for business purposes, or required by law. When you delete your account, we will remove your personal and business data from our active systems after any necessary retention period unless otherwise required.

9. Children’s Privacy #

Uplinorr Plus is intended for users aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us to request removal.

10. Changes to This Policy #

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will publish the updated policy here with a new effective date. We recommend you review this page periodically.
